How the dog nose works

fact #1

Have you ever wondered why your dog's nose is wet? That's because of the mucus covering to help your dog smell.

fact #2

Even though in cartoons dogs are known for their ears, their sense of smell is their strongest.

fact #3

Dogs' noses are like a human's eyes. They help them see the world. Your dog knows that you are you because they recognise your scent.
